Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Burak Çiçek ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the Pebble Blocks mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store personal information. However, the third-party services we use may collect certain data automatically:

  • Google AdMob — used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), IP address, and general usage data to serve relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Firebase Analytics — used to understand how players interact with the App. Data collected is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Apple App Store — in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

How We Use Information

Information collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Display advertisements and fund the free version of the App
  • Understand general usage patterns to improve the App

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob & Firebase
  • Apple

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will ask for your permission before tracking your activity across other apps and websites for personalized advertising. You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any data collected by third-party services is subject to their respective retention policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the contact page.
